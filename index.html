Hi, I'm Binh Nguyen

I’m a New York-based graphic designer who loves intelligent design, illustration, and beautiful subject matter! When I first started out in the field, I was told that I should “try to create the best design with the fewest number of shapes,” and it quickly became my working mantra. Even though I call myself a minimalist, I am also creative and spontaneous with my designs, taking great pride in being a versatile artist.

My previous experiences include working with The New York Botanical Garden, Gartner, The Jersey Journal, and Hachette Book Group. I am also a freelance designer, with works including logos, illustrations, advertisement materials, and web design using HTML, CSS and Javascript.

My other hobbies are landscape photography, hiking, riding motorcycle and snowboarding!
